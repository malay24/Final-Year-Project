About Project

Audiobooks are voice recordings of the text of a book that you listen to rather than read. Audiobooks can be exact word-for-word versions of books or abridged versions. The change in the paradigm of education has made audiobooks a better option to lure students into learning. Therefore, we decided to address the problem using Data Science and Machine Learning to provide an efficient solution.
